About me

Hi there! I am Søren Sandager Sørensen, currently a postdoc at Aarhus University within the project Grammar of Everyday Life (PI: Jakob Steensig). I have worked with prosody and phonetics in interaction and response tokens/interjections in Danish, but I am also interested in grammar and phonology in general. I also like typology and theory, and I am eager to work with languages other than Danish, and other approaches than I have so far!

I wrote my PhD thesis (finished 2020) on The Prosody of Response Tokens in Danish, and I have been part of the research groups DanTIN (Danish Talk-in-Interaction) and Sounds of Language and Speech.

At the moment, I am open to discussions about possible collaborations and projects. My current position lasts until January 2023 and ideas for the future are welcome. Do not hesitate to contact me! Send email to linsss@cc.au.dk.

I am currently working on topics related to:
  • Copula drop and drop of har 'has' in Danish - and what it says about both phonology, syntax and action
  • Assessments and their syntax
  • The word fuck in Danish interaction
  • Response tokens and other one-word-constructions
