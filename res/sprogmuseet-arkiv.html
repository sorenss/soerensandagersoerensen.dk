Archive of Sprogmuseet

Sprogmuseet was a Danish 00's style blog run mainly by Ole Stig Andersen with posts by many different people. The website started in 2009, published its last entry in 2015, and went offline in 2019. This archive contains PDF versions from the Internet Archive of all posts and other pages there (including displays of how it was hacked during its final days). After Oles death in 2019, the domain "Sprogmuseet.dk" was taken over by unaffiliated domain squatters, and therefore all links in the PDFs here have been removed. Lingoblog.dk is the spiritual successor, and some posts have also been republished there. There is also a partial Wordpress-based archive at sprogmuseet.schwa.dk and the blog posts by the DanTIN group at Samtalegrammatik.dk
